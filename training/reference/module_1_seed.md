## What to copy from this document
- **Voice:** intelligent-adult tone, dry where warranted, never preachy.
  No "harassment is bad, m'kay" energy. No safety-poster clichés.
- **Structure:** numbered sections; short framing intro; three-point
  highlight when something is commonly misunderstood; explicit
  "what you need to take from this module" close.
- **Length:** ~12 minutes reading + 8-question test served from a 25-question bank.
- **Scenarios:** plausible UK finance contexts (manager passing someone
  over after a declined date, client lunch with a derogatory comment,
  Teams DMs after a relationship ends, Friday team drinks).
- **Question design:** 80% pass mark, two retakes, `[scenario]` tag on
  the questions weighted to surface at least twice per attempt.
- **Always address the meta-fear directly:** "it was just banter" and
  "I didn't mean it" are confronted in body copy, not buried.
